Internet Service Providers

The ISP List is a database of Internet service and other online content providers that will help you get the information you need for your case. For each provider listed you will find legal contact info and instructions on how to serve subpoenas, court orders, and search warrants.  

The ISP List is a law enforcement community effort. If you are unable to find an ISP, let us know and we'll add it. If you have any questions ciphering results, contact us and we will try to answer all your questions.
